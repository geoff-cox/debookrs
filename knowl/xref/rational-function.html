Definition A.4. Rational Function.

A rational function is a function that is the division of two polynomials.
For example, the following are rational functions:
\begin{equation*} \frac{s^2 + 3s + 2}{s - 1}, \quad \frac{2s^3 - 5s + 7}{s^2 - 4}, \quad \frac{1}{s^2 + 1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
