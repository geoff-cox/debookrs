Identifying Nonlinear Terms.

A term is nonlinear if it contains a dependent variable, \(y\) or \(y^{(n)}\text{,}\) that is
  • raised to a power other than 1 \(\ds \left(\ y^2,\ (y')^{-4},\ \sqrt{y}\ \right)\text{.}\)
  • inside another function \(\ds \left(\ \ln(y),\ \sin(y),\ e^{y'}\ \right)\text{.}\)
  • multiplied or divided by another \(y\) or \(y^{(n)}\) \(\ds \left(\ y y''',\ y'/y\ \right)\text{.}\)
