    (i) Select-the-Best-Answer.
    Identify the linearity of the differential equation
    \begin{equation*} y'' + \sin(y) = 17t \text{.} \end{equation*}
    🔗
    • Linear
    • No, this is nonlinear. Looking carefully at each term, we see:
      \begin{gather*} y'' + \sin(y) = 17t \\ \underset{\text{linear}}{\underline{(1){\color{BurntOrange} y'' }}} + \underset{\text{nonlinear}}{\underline{\sin({\color{BurntOrange} y})}} = \underset{\text{linear}}{\underline{17{\color{BurntOrange} t}}} \end{gather*}
      Since one term is not linear, the entire differential equation is nonlinear.
    • Nonlinear
