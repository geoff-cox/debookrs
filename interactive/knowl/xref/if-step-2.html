Step 2: Multiply by \(\mu\) to Complete the Derivative

Multiply both sides of equation (24) by \(\mu\) and reverse the product rule on the left side.
\begin{align} \mu\frac{dy}{dx} + P(x) \mu y \amp = Q(x) \mu\tag{25}\\ \frac{d}{dx}\left[\mu \cdot y\right] \amp = Q(x) \mu\text{.}\tag{26} \end{align}
