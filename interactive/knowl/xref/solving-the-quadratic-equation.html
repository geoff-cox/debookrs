The solution to the quadratic equation
\begin{equation} a x^2 + b x + c = 0\tag{0} \end{equation}
is given by the quadratic formula:
\begin{equation} x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{\DLBb b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\text{.}\tag{0} \end{equation}
